# Second "Crusade"

The second Ottoman war is coming up. He no longer has Tunis as an ally. Since Tunis was the crusade target, we will miss out on the combat bonuses, so maybe we will just declare an additional war.

[[Qara Qoyunlu]] is looking strong with 64K troops and willing to ally me, so I'll curry favours until I can call them in to make the war a lot easier. Egypt is strong as well but they have a truce remaining. Also, we want to avoid allying Egypt as conquering them is vital to rush towards the Middle East and India as soon as possible.

[[Great Britain]] formed, turned [[Anglican]], and since I kept relations high and curried favors in the meantime, I am able to ally them again to call them in against the Ottomans.

I plan to park a lot of troops in [[Constantinople]] and immediately cross the strait before his ships can block me. If we manage to occupy the other side they will not be able to block our crossing. If this fails, I plan to keep a big stack around [[Constantinople]] and move another big stack all the way around to siege down a province.
