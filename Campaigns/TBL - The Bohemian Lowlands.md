## Strategy

I plan to use a diplomatic style with lots of subjects. [[Personal Union]]s early, using the [[Bohemia|Bohemian]] Mission Tree for [[Hungary]], [[Poland]] and [[Lithuania]] and hunting aggressively for additional free unions. Depending on lucky free unions, we can form nations to get missions to [[Personal Union|PU]] other nations. A Burgundian [[Personal Union|PU]] (or even direct inheritance) is very likely to succeed as well.

# One Religion

Having many subjects and picking [[Religious Ideas]] increases the likelihood of subjects picking these ideas too. Taking subjects good at converting helps too (e.g. [[Najd]], [[Jerusalem]]). Once we have many subjects, we can grant them land so that they convert it for us (and we can help them too).

# One Culture

We can reduce separatism using missions from [[Croatia]]. Then stack a few discount modifiers. Combined with Enlightenment Institution and Adjacency we will likely hit -90% without much effort.

# HRE

We will become Emperor mainly for the increased manpower, to be able to execute special military operations in Europe and slowly pass reforms which will give us some nice diplomatic benefits. Late-game we will try to disband the [[Holy Roman Empire|HRE]], either through annexing every member or losing emperorship on purpose.
