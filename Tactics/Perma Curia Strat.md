# Goal
Strategy to become [[Curia Controller]] and permanently keep it.

# Credits
Sourced from [[Lambdaxx]]'s [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CrEzDjL80Xo&pp=ygUPZXU0IHBlcm1hIGN1cmlh0gcJCdoKAYcqIYzv).

# Requirements
- Must:
	- Have a non-[[Catholic]] [[Subject]]
	- Have one or more [[Cardinal]]s
	- Have 5 or more [[Papal Influence]]
	- [[Papal States]] must have a Coastal Province
		- We use [[Terracina]] in this guide
- Nice
	- The [[Pope]] is very old
	- The [[Papal States]] is small

# Steps
1. Become [[Curia Controller]]
	- Declare war on the [[Papal States]]
	- Fully occupy, transfer [[Terracina]] occupation to your non-[[Catholic]] [[Subject]]
	- Wait until the [[Pope]] dies
	- Pause time
	- Invest 5 [[Papal Influence|PI]] for [[Curia Controller]]
	- Peace out with Full Annexation
	- Feed all provinces except [[Roma|Rome]] to your non-[[Catholic]] [[Subject]]
	- Accept [[Roma|Rome]]'s Papal Rebel Demands, re-creating the [[Papal States]]
		- This results in you becoming [[Curia Controller]]
2. Creating non-[[Catholic]] [[Papal States]]
	- Convert [[Terracina]] for your subject to it's religion
	- Wait for Truce with [[Papal States]] to expire
	- Seize [[Terracina]]
	- Accept [[Terracina]]'s Papal Rebel Demands
	- Declare war on the [[Papal States]] and fully Annex them
	- Feed [[Terracina]] to your subject
	- Accept [[Roma|Rome]]'s Papal Rebel Demands

# Explanation
The crucial step is to create a [[Papal States]] that is not Catholic. Many traditional ways of (re)creating non-Catholic countries do not work for the [[Papal States]], but it seems that accepting Rebel Demands still allow the Pope to have other religions.

The religion of a released country depends in some way on the religion of the most recently gained province. Therefore, the strategy is to feed the [[Papal States]] a non-Catholic province, then re-spawning them using Rebels.

It is likely that development also plays a role, but it is not clear to which extend exactly.

Using [[Birding]], the war can be kept short by reloading if the old Pope does not die.
