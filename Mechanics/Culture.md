# Culture Switching

## Button
Click the button, requires to have >= 50% of your states to contain the culture.

# Event
By having ruler and heir of one different culture you can trigger [[The Court Of Ruler.Name]] event, allowing you to change primary culture.

## Tall Culture Escape

If you currently have a "tall" primary culture (meaning your [[Capital]] state contains very high development) you can switch out by looking for a wide culture, meaning a culture with a lot of provinces. Some European examples are Francien, Ruthenian, English or Polish.
