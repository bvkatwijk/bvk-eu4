A subject type tied to a ruler. Only applicable for Christian nations. If the senior partner dies, the junior partner can break free under certain conditions.

# Strategies

## Young Ruler with Heir
If a nation has a young ruler (e.g. 15 years old) and they have an heir, it is more likely that their ruler dies when their heir is old, meaning that they are a long-term target for a PU.

## Switch Dynasty
Instead of getting your dynasty on another country's throne, it is sometimes easier to get their dynasty on your own throne. You can then PU them easier. Very useful to switch to [[Trastamara Dynasty]] for example, since that makes [[Castille]], [[Aragon]] and [[Naples]] suitable targets.

### Switch during war
If you are in a Force Union war, you can change to a different dynasty and still pick the Force Union peace deal option.
