# Move Capital to Colonial Region

To move your capital to a colonial region, it must
- be the only stated province
- have no land or strait connection to your other provinces

A workaround is to move the capital first to a different continent (non-colonial) which doesn't have the above requirements. Now your new capital meets the criteria, meaning you will now be able to move it to the colonial region.
