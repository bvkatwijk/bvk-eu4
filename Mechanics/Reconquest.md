# Tips

## Set Up

Early game in a war against a medium-sized nation, call in his neighbours and give away all his land and vassalize him. This reduces [[Aggressive Expansion|AE]] and Diplomatic Power costs and lets you reconquest the lost land later for only 25% [[Aggressive Expansion|AE]].

This move can even allow you to give so much [[Aggressive Expansion|AE]] to the ally that you can create a coalition on them, which can result in them having to return the land to your vassal.

In the [[Holy Roman Empire|HRE]], the Emperor will often demand the lost land to be returned your new vassal.
## Cede War Goal

In a Reconquest war, consider ceding the war goal (as opposed to return core) because it is often less war score.
