# Un-state before forming

Before clicking decision to form a tag, you can un-state the provinces of the primary [[Culture]]. When you now form a tag that moves your [[Capital]] (for example [[Sardinia-Piedmont]]), you will have your [[Capital]] outside of a state, making future tag switching a lot easier.
