# No Aggressive Expansion

Countries that you cannot see do not get [[Aggressive Expansion|AE]] towards you. So expanding hard towards the fog of war can save a lot of [[Aggressive Expansion|AE]] and help you keep expanding.

# Troop Count of Unknown Countries

Using their Allies, you can see the troops of countries that you have not discovered yet by opening the Declare War UI. For example, we can see that [[Ajam]] has 16K infantry even though we have not discovered them yet:

# Semi-Discovered

Sometimes you have discovered a province belonging to a country that you have not discovered yet. You can now discover the country by clicking on the province. Best to avoid this if you want to benefit from not having them discovered.
