Morale is the willingness of your troops to continue the fight. If Morale is zero,  troops will either retreat or be [[Combat|stackwipe]]d.

Troops inflict Morale damage, meaning they reduce the morale of the opposing troops. The amount of morale damage depends on your maximum Morale.
